　明智的臣僕是君王所重用的；貽羞的僕人，必觸動他的怒氣。

Prov 14：35（KJV）

　The king's favour is toward a wise servant: but his wrath is against him that causeth shame.

Prov 14：35（NIV）

　A king delights in a wise servant, but a shameful servant incurs his wrath.
