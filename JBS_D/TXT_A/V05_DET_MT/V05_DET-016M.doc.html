申命記 第16章三種節期

經文表解（申16:1-22）

來朝見神

不可空手

一、逾越節的條例

二、七七節的條例

三、住柵節的條例

1.領出埃及

2.喫無酵餅

3.守嚴肅守

1.收割莊稼

2.獻甘心祭

3.一同歡樂

1.穀酒收藏

2.守節七日

3.賜福一切

經文彙編（申16:1-22）

申16:1（UCV）【逾越節】你要注意亞筆月，向耶和華你的　神守逾越節；因為耶和華你的　神在亞筆月夜間，領你出埃及。

申16:1（NCV）【逾越節（出12:1-20）】你要守亞筆月，向耶和華你的　神守逾越節，因為耶和華你的　神在亞筆月的一個晚上，把你從埃及地領出來。

申16:1（CLV）要常常記著守逾越節，因為主在一月的一個晚上把你帶出埃及。

Deut 16:1（KJV）Observe the month of Abib, and keep the passover unto the LORD thy God: for in the month of Abib the LORD thy God brought thee forth out of Egypt by night.

Deut 16:1（NIV）【Passover】Observe the month of Abib and celebrate the Passover of the LORD your God, because in the month of Abib he brought you out of Egypt by night.

申16:2（UCV）你當在耶和華所選擇要立為祂名的居所，從牛群、羊群中，將逾越節的祭牲獻給耶和華你的　神。

申16:2（NCV）你要在耶和華選擇立為他名的居所的地方，從羊和牛作逾越節的祭牲獻給耶和華你的　神。

申16:2（CLV）你要在上帝選定的地方，就是祂的聖所，獻上牛或羊作逾越節的祭物。

Deut 16:2（KJV） Thou shalt therefore sacrifice the passover unto the LORD thy God, of the flock and the herd, in the place which the LORD shall choose to place his name there.

Deut 16:2（NIV）Sacrifice as the Passover to the LORD your God an animal from your flock or herd at the place the LORD will choose as a dwelling for his Name.

申16:3（UCV）你吃這祭牲，不可吃有酵的餅，七日之內要吃無酵餅，就是困苦餅（你本是急忙出了埃及地）；要叫你一生一世記念你從埃及地出來的日子。

申16:3（NCV）你吃這祭的時候，不可和有酵的餅一同吃；七日之內，你要吃無酵餅，就是困苦餅，因為你是急急忙忙從埃及地出來的，好使你一生的年日都可以記念你從埃及地出來的日子。

申16:3（CLV）吃祭物的時候，也要吃無酵餅；你要吃無酵餅七天，目的是提醒你當時是怎樣倉猝地離開埃及。你一生一世都要記念那日子。

Deut 16:3（KJV）Thou shalt eat no leavened bread with it; seven days shalt thou eat unleavened bread therewith, even the bread of affliction; for thou camest forth out of the land of Egypt in haste: that thou mayest remember the day when thou camest forth out of the land of Egypt all the days of thy life.

Deut 16:3（NIV）Do not eat it with bread made with yeast, but for seven days eat unleavened bread, the bread of affliction, because you left Egypt in haste--so that all the days of your life you may remember the time of your departure from Egypt.

申16:4（UCV）在你四境之內，七日不可見麵酵；頭一日晚上所獻的肉，一點不可留到早晨。

申16:4（NCV）七天之內，在你的全境不可見有酵。第一天晚上你獻的祭肉，一點也不可剩下，留到早晨。

申16:4（CLV）七日之內，你周圍不可有酵母；獻祭的肉，不可留到第二天。

Deut 16:4（KJV）And there shall be no leavened bread seen with thee in all thy coast seven days; neither shall there any thing of the flesh, which thou sacrificedst the first day at even, remain all night until the morning.

Deut 16:4（NIV）Let no yeast be found in your possession in all your land for seven days. Do not let any of the meat you sacrifice on the evening of the first day remain until morning.

申16:5（UCV）在耶和華你　神所賜的各城中，你不可獻逾越節的祭；

申16:5（NCV）你不能在耶和華你的　神賜給你的任何一座城裏，宰殺逾越節的祭牲；

申16:5（CLV）你不可在自己的地方獻逾越節的祭物，

Deut 16:5（KJV）Thou mayest not sacrifice the passover within any of thy gates, which the LORD thy God giveth thee:

Deut 16:5（NIV）You must not sacrifice the Passover in any town the LORD your God gives you

申16:6（UCV）只當在耶和華你　神所選擇要立為他名的居所，晚上日落的時候，乃是你出埃及的時候，獻逾越節的祭。

申16:6（NCV）只當在耶和華你的　神選擇作他名的居所的地方，晚上日落的時候，就是你出埃及的時刻，宰殺逾越節的祭牲；

申16:6（CLV）在日落的時候，你應當在上帝選定的地方，就是祂的聖所，獻上祭物，記念你離開埃及的日子。

Deut 16:6（KJV）But at the place which the LORD thy God shall choose to place his name in, there thou shalt sacrifice the passover at even, at the going down of the sun, at the season that thou camest forth out of Egypt.

Deut 16:6（NIV）except in the place he will choose as a dwelling for his Name. There you must sacrifice the Passover in the evening, when the sun goes down, on the anniversary of your departure from Egypt.

申16:7（UCV）當在耶和華你　神所選擇的地方把肉烤了吃〔『烤』或作『煮』〕，次日早晨就回到你的帳棚去。

申16:7（NCV）你要在耶和華你的　神選擇的地方，把祭肉煮了吃；到了早晨，你才可以回到家裏去。

申16:7（CLV）你要在那裡煮吃祭物，次日早上就可以返回自己的家。

Deut 16:7（KJV）And thou shalt roast and eat it in the place which the LORD thy God shall choose: and thou shalt turn in the morning, and go unto thy tents.

Deut 16:7（NIV）Roast it and eat it at the place the LORD your God will choose. Then in the morning return to your tents.

申16:8（UCV）你要吃無酵餅六日，第七日要向耶和華你的　神守嚴肅會，不可作工。

申16:8（NCV）六日之內你要吃無酵餅；到了第七日，要向耶和華你的　神守聖會；甚麼工都不可作。

申16:8（CLV）在隨著來的六天之內，你們不可吃有酵的餅，第七天要在上帝面前肅靜聚集。那一天你不可做任何的事。

Deut 16:8（KJV）Six days thou shalt eat unleavened bread: and on the seventh day shall be a solemn assembly to the LORD thy God: thou shalt do no work therein.

Deut 16:8（NIV）For six days eat unleavened bread and on the seventh day hold an assembly to the LORD your God and do no work.

申16:9（UCV）【七七節】你要計算七七日，從你開鐮收割禾稼時算起，共計七七日。

申16:9（NCV）【七七節（出34:22、利23:15-21）】你要計算七個七日，從開鐮收割禾稼的時候算起，共計七個七日。

申16:9（CLV）從收割那天開始，數七個星期，

Deut 16:9（KJV）Seven weeks shalt thou number unto thee: begin to number the seven weeks from such time as thou beginnest to put the sickle to the corn.

Deut 16:9（NIV）【Feast of Weeks】Count off seven weeks from the time you begin to put the sickle to the standing grain.

申16:10（UCV）你要照耶和華你　神所賜你的福，手裏拿著甘心祭，獻在耶和華你的　神面前，守七七節，

申16:10（NCV）你要向耶和華你的　神舉行七七節，照著耶和華你的　神賜福你的，盡你所能的獻上你手裏的甘心祭。

申16:10（CLV）然後，當在主面前慶祝七七節。那時，你們要按著主賜福你所得到的收成，甘心樂意地獻上祭物。

Deut 16:10（KJV）And thou shalt keep the feast of weeks unto the LORD thy God with a tribute of a freewill offering of thine hand, which thou shalt give unto the LORD thy God, according as the LORD thy God hath blessed thee:

Deut 16:10（NIV）Then celebrate the Feast of Weeks to the LORD your God by giving a freewill offering in proportion to the blessings the LORD your God has given you.

申16:11（UCV）你和你兒女、僕婢，並住在你城裏的利未人，以及在你們中間寄居的與孤兒、寡婦，都要在耶和華你　神所選擇立為他名的居所，在耶和華你的　神面前歡樂。

申16:11（NCV）你和你的兒女、僕婢，以及在你城裏的利未人、在你們中間的寄居者、孤兒和寡婦，都要在耶和華你的　神選擇作他名的居所的地方歡樂。

申16:11（CLV）你一家都應當歡喜快樂，但不要忘記在你境內的利未人、外族人、寡婦和孤兒；應當邀請他們在主的聖所與你一起慶祝。

Deut 16:11（KJV）And thou shalt rejoice before the LORD thy God, thou, and thy son, and thy daughter, and thy manservant, and thy maidservant, and the Levite that is within thy gates, and the stranger, and the fatherless, and the widow, that are among you, in the place which the LORD thy God hath chosen to place his name there.

Deut 16:11（NIV）And rejoice before the LORD your God at the place he will choose as a dwelling for his Name--you, your sons and daughters, your menservants and maidservants, the Levites in your towns, and the aliens, the fatherless and the widows living among you.

申16:12（UCV）你也要記念你在埃及作過奴僕。你要謹守遵行這些律例。

申16:12（NCV）你也要記得你在埃及作過奴僕；你要謹守遵行這些律例。

申16:12（CLV）要記著你當日在埃及也是作奴隸的。你要遵守這些命令。

Deut 16:12（KJV）And thou shalt remember that thou wast a bondman in Egypt: and thou shalt observe and do these statutes.

Deut 16:12（NIV）Remember that you were slaves in Egypt, and follow carefully these decrees.

申16:13（UCV）【住棚節】你把禾場的穀、酒醡的酒收藏以後，就要守住棚節七日。

申16:13（NCV）【住棚節（利23:33-43）】你從禾場上和榨酒池裏收藏了出產以後，就要舉行住棚節七日。

申16:13（CLV）在收成以後，要守七天的住棚節。

Deut 16:13（KJV）Thou shalt observe the feast of tabernacles seven days, after that thou hast gathered in thy corn and thy wine:

Deut 16:13（NIV）【Feast of Tabernacles】 Celebrate the Feast of Tabernacles for seven days after you have gathered the produce of your threshing floor and your winepress.

申16:14（UCV）守節的時候，你和你兒女、僕婢，並住在你城裏的利未人，以及寄居的與孤兒寡婦，都要歡樂。

申16:14（NCV）在這節期以內，你和你的兒女、僕婢，以及住在你城裏的利未人、寄居者、孤兒和寡婦，都要歡樂。

申16:14（CLV）這是你和你的兒女、僕人都應該快樂的節日。但不要忘記城中的利未人、外族人、孤兒和寡婦。

Deut 16:14（KJV）And thou shalt rejoice in thy feast, thou, and thy son, and thy daughter, and thy manservant, and thy maidservant, and the Levite, the stranger, and the fatherless, and the widow, that are within thy gates.

Deut 16:14（NIV） Be joyful at your Feast--you, your sons and daughters, your menservants and maidservants, and the Levites, the aliens, the fatherless and the widows who live in your towns.

申16:15（UCV）在耶和華所選擇的地方，你當向耶和華你的　神守節七日；因為耶和華你　神在你一切的土產上，和你手裏所辦的事上要賜福與你，你就非常地歡樂。

申16:15（NCV）你要在耶和華選擇的地方，向耶和華你的　神守節七日，因為耶和華你的　神，要在你一切土產上，和你手所作的一切事上，賜福給你，你就滿有歡樂。

申16:15（CLV）這宴會要在主的聖所舉行。你應當衷心感謝主，因為祂賜福，使你有好的收成，又在其他的事上賜福你，所以，你應該滿心歡樂。

Deut 16:15（KJV）Seven days shalt thou keep a solemn feast unto the LORD thy God in the place which the LORD shall choose: because the LORD thy God shall bless thee in all thine increase, and in all the works of thine hands, therefore thou shalt surely rejoice.

Deut 16:15（NIV）For seven days celebrate the Feast to the LORD your God at the place the LORD will choose. For the LORD your God will bless you in all your harvest and in all the work of your hands, and your joy will be complete.

申16:16（UCV）你一切的男丁要在除酵節、七七節、住棚節，一年三次，在耶和華你　神所選擇的地方朝見祂，卻不可空手朝見。

申16:16（NCV）每年三次，就是在除酵節、七七節、住棚節，你所有的男丁都要在耶和華選擇的地方，朝見耶和華你的　神；但不可空手朝見耶和華。

申16:16（CLV）每年三次，你的男丁要來到主的聖所，就是在無酵節、七七節和住棚節。但不可空手而來，

Deut 16:16（NIV）Three times a year all your men must appear before the LORD your God at the place he will choose: at the Feast of Unleavened Bread, the Feast of Weeks and the Feast of Tabernacles. No man should appear before the LORD empty-handed:

申16:17（NCV）各人要按手中的力量，照著耶和華你的　神賜你的福，奉獻禮物。

Deut 16:17（NIV）Each of you must bring a gift in proportion to the way the LORD your God has blessed you.

申16:18（NCV）【設立審判官與官長】在耶和華你的　神賜給你的各城裏，你要按著各支派設立審判官和官長；他們要按著公義的判斷審判人民。

申16:18（CLV）當在主分給你的土地上，按著宗族，委任審判官和治理的官員；他們要按公義治理各地。

Deut 16:18（KJV）Judges and officers shalt thou make thee in all thy gates, which the LORD thy God giveth thee, throughout thy tribes: and they shall judge the people with just judgment.

Deut 16:18（NIV）【Judges】Appoint judges and officials for each of your tribes in every town the LORD your God is giving you, and they shall judge the people fairly.

申16:19（NCV）不可屈枉正直，不可徇人的情面，不可收受賄賂，因為賄賂能使智慧人的眼變瞎，也能使義人的話顛倒過來。

申16:19（CLV）他們不可歪曲正直，枉法偏私；也不可收受賄賂，因為賄賂能蒙蔽聰明人的眼睛，使人作出錯誤的判斷。

Deut 16:20（NIV）Follow justice and justice alone, so that you may live and possess the land the LORD your God is giving you.
