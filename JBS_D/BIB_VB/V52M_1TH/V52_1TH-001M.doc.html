Thanksgiving for Thessalonians' Faith

（1Th 1:2-10）

1_Thes 1:2（KJV）We give thanks to God always for you all, making mention of you in our prayers;

1_Thes 1:2（NIV）We always thank God for all of you, mentioning you in our prayers.

1_Thes 1:3（KJV）Remembering without ceasing your work of faith, and labour of love, and patience of hope in our Lord Jesus Christ, in the sight of God and our Father;

1_Thes 1:3（NIV）We continually remember before our God and Father your work produced by faith, your labor prompted by love, and your endurance inspired by hope in our Lord Jesus Christ.

1_Thes 1:4（KJV）Knowing, brethren beloved, your election of God.

1_Thes 1:4（NIV）For we know, brothers loved by God, that he has chosen you,

1_Thes 1:5（KJV）For our gospel came not unto you in word only, but also in power, and in the Holy Ghost, and in much assurance; as ye know what manner of men we were among you for your sake.

1_Thes 1:5（NIV）because our gospel came to you not simply with words, but also with power, with the Holy Spirit and with deep conviction. You know how we lived among you for your sake.

1_Thes 1:6（KJV）And ye became followers of us, and of the Lord, having received the word in much affliction, with joy of the Holy Ghost:

1_Thes 1:6（NIV）You became imitators of us and of the Lord; in spite of severe suffering, you welcomed the message with the joy given by the Holy Spirit.

1_Thes 1:7（KJV）So that ye were ensamples to all that believe in Macedonia and Achaia.

1_Thes 1:7（NIV）And so you became a model to all the believers in Macedonia and Achaia.

1_Thes 1:8（KJV）For from you sounded out the word of the Lord not only in Macedonia and Achaia, but also in every place your faith to God-ward is spread abroad; so that we need not to speak any thing.

1_Thes 1:8（NIV）The Lord's message rang out from you not only in Macedonia and Achaia--your faith in God has become known everywhere. Therefore we do not need to say anything about it,

1_Thes 1:9（KJV）For they themselves shew of us what manner of entering in we had unto you, and how ye turned to God from idols to serve the living and true God;

1_Thes 1:9（NIV）for they themselves report what kind of reception you gave us. They tell how you turned to God from idols to serve the living and true God,

1_Thes 1:10（KJV）And to wait for his Son from heaven, whom he raised from the dead, even Jesus, which delivered us from the wrath to come.

1_Thes 1:10（NIV）and to wait for his Son from heaven, whom he raised from the dead--Jesus, who rescues us from the coming wrath.
